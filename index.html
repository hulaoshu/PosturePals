Common Posture Mistakes (We've All Been There!)

Check out these real examples of bad posture - can you spot what's wrong?

‚ùå Bad

The Desk Slouch

What's wrong: Rounded shoulders, forward head, curved back

Why it's bad: Causes neck strain, back pain, and compressed lungs

Fix it: Sit with feet flat, back straight, screen at eye level

‚ùå Bad

Text Neck

What's wrong: Head tilted down, neck bent forward

Why it's bad: Puts 60 pounds of pressure on your neck! Can cause permanent damage

Fix it: Hold phone at eye level, take breaks, stretch your neck

‚ùå Bad

The Leg Crosser

What's wrong: One leg crossed over the other, uneven hips

Why it's bad: Misaligns your spine and can cause hip and back pain

Fix it: Keep both feet flat on the floor, switch positions regularly

‚úÖ Good

Perfect Posture!

What's right: Straight back, shoulders back, head aligned

Why it's great: Reduces strain, improves breathing, boosts confidence

Keep it up: This is what we're aiming for! Your PostureGotchi will love this!
